Portfolio Dashboard

Your investments, beautifully organized

Altruist's portfolio view is built for clarity — a clean, modern interface that shows your account values, holdings, and performance without the noise of a traditional brokerage platform. Everything you need, nothing you don't.

  • Real-time account balances and total portfolio value
  • Clean holdings breakdown with current value and performance
  • Cost basis and unrealized gain/loss for every position
  • Performance tracking from account inception
Transaction History

Every transaction, clearly recorded

Altruist's transaction history is clean and navigable — trades, dividends, interest, deposits, and withdrawals, all organized chronologically with filtering by date, account, and transaction type.

  • Complete trade and dividend history by account
  • Deposits, withdrawals, and transfers clearly labeled
  • Filter by date range, account, and transaction type
  • Downloadable for your own records and tax prep
