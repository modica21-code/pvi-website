Privacy Policy

How Pine Valley Investments collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: March 2026

01

Introduction

Pine Valley Investments, LLC ("PVI," "we," "us," or "our") is a Registered Investment Advisor (RIA) registered with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website at pinevalleyinvestments.com (the "Site"), submit forms, or otherwise interact with us online. Please read this policy carefully. By using our Site, you agree to the practices described herein.

This policy does not govern the collection or use of information by the third-party financial platforms and portals linked from our Site (such as Charles Schwab, Altruist, eMoney, RightCapital, Tamarac, or Monarch Money). Those platforms maintain their own privacy practices, which we encourage you to review.

02

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us through forms on our Site, as well as certain information collected automatically when you visit.

Information you provide directly:

  • Name, email address, and phone number submitted through contact or consultation request forms
  • Messages or inquiries submitted to a specific advisor via bio page contact forms
  • Email address submitted to subscribe to the Morning Core newsletter
  • Any other information you choose to include in a message or form submission

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring URLs
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns
  • Device type and screen resolution
  • Cookie identifiers and similar tracking technologies (see Section 5)
03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries, consultation requests, or messages submitted through our contact forms
  • To route advisor-specific form submissions to the appropriate Pine Valley Investments advisor
  • To deliver the Morning Core newsletter to subscribers who have opted in
  • To analyze Site traffic and user behavior in order to improve the Site's content, performance, and user experience
  • To comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations as a Registered Investment Advisor
  • To protect the security and integrity of our Site and services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

04

Form Data & Third-Party Services

Contact forms on our Site — including bio page advisor contact forms and the main Contact Us form — are processed through Formspree (formspree.io). When you submit a form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers and delivered to Pine Valley Investments via email. Formspree's privacy policy is available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

We use or may use the following third-party services in connection with our Site:

  • Formspree — contact form processing and email delivery
  • Mailchimp — Morning Core newsletter delivery and subscriber management
  • Google Analytics — Site traffic analysis and behavior reporting
  • Vercel — website hosting and content delivery
  • HubSpot — CRM and lead management (form submissions may be routed to HubSpot)

Each of these providers operates under its own privacy policy and data processing agreements. We encourage you to review their respective policies if you have questions about how they handle your data.

05

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our Site uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and collect analytics data. A cookie is a small text file placed on your device by your browser when you visit a website.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — Required for the Site to function. These include session management and security cookies. They cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — Used to understand how visitors interact with the Site (pages visited, time on page, traffic sources). We use Google Analytics for this purpose. These cookies are only set if you accept our cookie consent banner.
  • Preference cookies — Store your cookie consent choice so we do not ask you again on subsequent visits.

You may decline non-essential cookies using the cookie consent banner displayed when you first visit the Site. You can also manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features.

06

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law and regulation.

  • Form submissions (contact and consultation requests) are retained in our email systems and CRM for as long as necessary to manage the client or prospect relationship
  • Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data is retained in accordance with Google Analytics' standard data retention settings

As a Registered Investment Advisor, Pine Valley Investments is subject to SEC recordkeeping requirements under Rule 204-2 of the Investment Advisers Act of 1940, which may require us to retain certain records for defined periods regardless of the above.

07

Information Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Our Site is served over HTTPS, and form submissions are transmitted securely.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We encourage you to use caution when sharing sensitive financial information through any online channel.

08

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of data we hold about you.

  • Access & correction — You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to our legal and regulatory retention obligations
  • Opt-out of newsletter — You may unsubscribe from the Morning Core newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any newsletter email
  • Cookie preferences — You may update your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser's local storage and revisiting the Site

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at mmodica@pinevalleyinvestments.com or by mail at the address below.

09

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information. You have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion of your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. Pine Valley Investments does not sell personal information.

To submit a request under the CCPA, please contact us using the information in Section 11 below.

10

Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12
