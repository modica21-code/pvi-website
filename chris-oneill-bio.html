About Chris

Chris O'Neill joined Pine Valley Investments in 2022 as Chief Investment Officer, bringing institutional-grade rigor to PVI's investment management platform. As CIO, Chris leads the firm's investment committee, oversees portfolio construction, manager due diligence, and risk management across all client accounts.

Chris holds the CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) and CIPM (Certificate in Investment Performance Measurement) designations — among the most respected credentials in the investment profession. His approach combines quantitative analysis with a deep understanding of how markets behave over full market cycles.

Prior to PVI, Chris spent his career in institutional investment management, where he developed the frameworks and discipline he now applies on behalf of the firm's clients. He believes that transparency, accountability, and evidence-based decision-making are the cornerstones of sound investment management.
