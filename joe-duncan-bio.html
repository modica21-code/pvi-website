Leadership

Joseph Duncan

COO & Founding Partner

The best client relationships are built on trust earned over decades — not promised in a pitch.

Joseph Duncan is a co-founder and COO of Pine Valley Investments, bringing decades of wealth management experience to the firm he helped build from the ground up. Joe's steady hand on firm operations and his deep-rooted client relationships have been central to PVI's growth from a $30 million startup to a $2.6 billion enterprise.

As Chief Operating Officer, Joe oversees the day-to-day functioning of the firm, ensuring that PVI's processes, technology, and people are aligned to deliver an exceptional client experience. His philosophy is simple: great outcomes come from getting the fundamentals right, every single day.

Joe is based in the Philadelphia region and has spent his entire career serving families and business owners navigating complex financial decisions. His clients describe him as steady, thoughtful, and genuinely invested in their success.
