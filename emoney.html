Account Aggregation

Every account, one dashboard

eMoney connects to thousands of financial institutions, pulling your balances, holdings, and transactions into a single organized view. Your advisor sees the same dashboard — enabling advice that accounts for your complete financial picture, not just what we manage.

  • Connects to 11,000+ financial institutions automatically
  • Investment, banking, credit, and real estate all in one place
  • Shared real-time view with your Pine Valley advisor
  • Secure read-only connections — we never move money
Long-Term Projections

See your wealth grow over a lifetime

Lifetime Portfolio Value modeling shows how your assets could perform across your full financial timeline — from today through retirement and beyond. Your advisor uses this to illustrate the long-term impact of every planning decision.

  • Projected portfolio growth tied to your actual accounts
  • Scenario modeling for different savings and spending rates
  • Visualize the long-term impact of Roth conversions and distributions
  • Updated live as your plan and assumptions evolve
Foundational Planning

Your plan, at a glance

The eMoney Plan Summary puts your financial health front and center — showing income, expenses, savings, and goal progress in one clear view. You and your advisor can quickly identify gaps and opportunities together, then act on them in the same session.

  • At-a-glance financial health summary
  • Income, expense, and savings rate breakdown
  • Goal funding status across retirement, education, and more
  • Live updates as your advisor refines your plan
Monte Carlo Analysis

Know your odds before you retire

eMoney's Monte Carlo engine runs thousands of market scenarios against your actual plan to calculate your probability of success. It's the same institutional analysis used by top advisory firms — built into your complimentary PVI client portal.
