About the Platform

The portal built for your financial life

eMoney is a comprehensive financial planning platform used by advisors and clients together. Through your personal portal, view all accounts, upload documents, monitor net worth in real time, and stay aligned with your advisor on every aspect of your financial plan.

  • Aggregates all financial accounts in a single secure view
  • Real-time net worth tracking across assets and liabilities
  • Document vault for estate, insurance, and tax files
  • Shared access with your Pine Valley advisor for live collaboration
What You Can Do

Powerful planning tools built for every stage

Plan Summary

See a clear, consolidated snapshot of your financial plan — income, expenses, assets, and liabilities — so you always know where you stand and what comes next.

Lifetime Portfolio Value

Track how your portfolio is projected to grow with interactive charts that model different scenarios, contribution rates, and spending assumptions over decades.

Monte Carlo Analysis

Run thousands of simulated market scenarios to understand the probability of your plan succeeding — and make smarter decisions about risk, spending, and savings.

Account Aggregation

Connect bank accounts, investments, loans, real estate, and insurance from thousands of institutions. Your full financial picture in one secure place.

Document Vault

Securely store and access important financial documents — wills, trust agreements, insurance policies, tax returns — organized and accessible whenever you need them.

Advisor Collaboration

Your Pine Valley advisor works inside the same portal. Review plans together, make updates in real time, and stay connected between annual reviews — no lag, no friction.

Foundational Planning

Everything in your plan,
organized clearly

The Plan Summary view gives you an instant snapshot of your financial health — income sources, recurring expenses, asset values, outstanding liabilities, and insurance coverage. It's the foundation your advisor builds every recommendation on.

  • Income, savings, and expense summary at a glance
  • Balance sheet view of all assets and liabilities
  • Insurance and coverage overview in one screen
  • Updated automatically as accounts sync
Long-Term Projections

See your wealth grow
over a lifetime

The Lifetime Portfolio Value chart projects the long-term trajectory of your assets based on your current plan. Visualize how contributions, withdrawals, and investment returns compound over time — and adjust assumptions to see the impact instantly.

  • Multi-decade portfolio growth visualization
  • Interactive sliders for contribution and return assumptions
  • Compares plan scenarios side by side
  • Helps your advisor align strategy to your legacy goals
Risk & Probability Analysis

Know the odds your
plan will succeed

Monte Carlo analysis runs thousands of simulated market scenarios to calculate the statistical probability of your plan reaching its goals. Rather than a single "average" projection, you see the full range of outcomes — and your advisor uses this to build a plan that holds up under real-world conditions.

  • Thousands of market simulations run automatically
  • Success probability displayed as a clear percentage
  • Identifies plan vulnerabilities before they become problems
  • Guides smarter conversations about risk and spending
