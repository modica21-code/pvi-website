Meet the Full Team

Chris serves as Chief Investment Officer (CIO) and Portfolio Manager for Pine Valley Investments. Chris oversees all aspects of investment management including portfolio construction/development, research, and trading. In addition, Chris manages PVI's investment management operations team responsible for strategy implementation and maintenance across the entire firm. Chris is a member of the firm's investment committee and the lead portfolio manager for PVI's single stock and Core ETFs strategies.

Prior to joining Pine Valley Investments (PVI) in 2019, Chris spent 10 years working for S&P Dow Jones Indexes. In his role as senior index manager, Chris was responsible for managing numerous indexes which underlie many well-known exchange traded funds. During his 10 years, Chris was responsible for managing the following index families: Dow Jones U.S Select Sectors, Dow Jones U.S Indexes, Dow Jones Composite Indexes, S&P GSCI, S&P Commodity Indexes, and S&P Strategy Indexes.

Chris holds the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) designation and the Certificate in Investment Performance Measurement (CIPM) designation, both awarded by the CFA Institute. He received his B.S. in Finance from Saint Joseph's University.
