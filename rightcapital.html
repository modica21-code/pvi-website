Education Funding

Know exactly what college will cost your family

RightCapital projects the real future cost of each child's education based on school type and time horizon. See funded vs. shortfall clearly, and model 529 contributions to close any gap.

  • Per-child projections with funded / shortfall breakdown
  • Goal coverage percentage that updates as contributions change
  • Fully integrated with your overall financial plan
  • Action Items guide your next steps toward full funding
Retirement Scenarios

Model every "what if" before you retire

Slide retirement age, adjust savings rates, change spending assumptions, and switch Social Security strategies — all with a Proposed vs. Current comparison that shows the impact of every decision.

  • Side-by-side Proposed vs. Current plan comparison
  • Optimal Social Security timing built into projections
  • Multiple distribution and debt strategies to compare
  • Five dynamic spending models including Guardrails and Smile
